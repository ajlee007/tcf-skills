### Writing Register Rules by Level
- A2: simple sentences, present/past tense, basic connectors (et, mais, parce que)
- B1: compound sentences, future/conditional, common discourse markers
- B2: complex argumentation, subjunctive, varied connectors, formal tone
- C1: sophisticated register, nominalization, inversion, rhetorical structure

When a TCF tutor request comes in:

1. **Identify the module** — Grammar / Vocabulary / Speaking / Reading / Writing

3. **Apply global output defaults** — markdown file, English translations, answer key at end
